About Me

Thank you for stopping by! I am a fourth-year student at Metropolia University of Applied Science, majoring in Information and Communications Technology, with a specialization in Mobile Solutions. My academic journey began with a degree in Fashion Design, which ignited a keen interest and passion for Full stack web development. This newfound passion led me to craft intuitive user interfaces and bring them to life for real-world users.

Throughout my study, I have learned various technologies such as backend development using Node.js, basics of mobile application development spanning Android, iOS, and React Native platforms. Additionally, I have gained basics of SQL and NoSQL(MongoDB) databases, honed my skills in version control with Git, grasped the concepts of CI/CD, and embraced the Agile methodology, encompassing design sprint, scrum practices.

My journey has been enriched by diverse experiences, both in local and international environments. Collaborating on various projects has nurtured qualities like open-mindedness, a people-centric approach, and rapid adaptability. I thrive when I grapple with vague requirements and enjoy the process of deciphering needs and transforming them into tangible solutions. The satisfaction of problem-solving drives me forward to meet and exceed requirements while ensuring the satisfaction of those I serve.
